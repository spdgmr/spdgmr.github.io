    What's our goals?

    Our goals is to preserve Nintendo Wii homebrew and make it accessible to anyone years later. It's already becoming VERY clear that this stuff is getting rarer and rarer, and a lot of media is already gone after Nintendo shut down the DarkUmbra hacking/piracy forum back in 2017.

    This sent a very strong message; this kind of media must be preserved. Efforts were already being made years ago but preservation wasn't considered as important back when the Wii was at its homebrew peak.

    How can I help?

    If you wish to support our efforts, you can take action by doing any of the following:

    • Submitting any rare .wad, .elf, .dol, or other Wii related media you find.
    • Reuploading our archives yourself everywhere possible.
    • Promoting Forwarder Factory, ie. sending people a link to this website.
